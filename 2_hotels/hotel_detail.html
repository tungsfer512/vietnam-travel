The Shanshui Trends Hotel (Beijing Tongzhou Huanqiu Liyuan Subway Station) was recently renovated in 2019, making it a fantastic choice for those staying in Beijing. The hotel is conveniently located just 4km from Tongzhou Railway Station and 33km from Beijing Capital International Airport. In addition, Liyuan Metro Station is just a short walk away. Famous local attractions Tongzhou Shengfangji Ge Tianzhu Church (qidaosuo), Niuyue International Children Club and Dong'aobingshang Club, can all be reached with a short walk. At the end of a busy day, travelers can unwind and relax in the hotel or go out and enjoy the city. This Beijing hotel features parking on site. Germaphobes can rest easy - the level of cleanliness is highly rated at this hotel. This hotel is particularly popular with those traveling on business.
